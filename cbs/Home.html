Ben Carson, in full Benjamin Solomon Carson ,Sr., [born september 18,1951,Detroit ,Michigan,U.S],American politician and neurosurgeon who performed the first succesful separation of conjoined twins who were attached at the back of the head [occipital craniopagus twins].The operation ,who took place in 1987 ,lasted some some 22hours and involved a 70 member surgical theme. Carson also refined a technique known as hemispherectomy, in which one half of the brain is removed to prevent seizures in persons with severe epilepsy.He later became active in politics and served as U.S secretary . Carson spent his childhood in Detroit .His parents divorced when he was eight ,he and thereafter ,he lived with with his mother and brother ,spending a brief period in Boston and later returning to Detroit .Although Carson showed potential as a student ,he performed poorly in school until his mother challenged him and his brother with reading and writing assignments in addition to their regular schoolwork. Carson developed a new found interest in learning and eventually earned a scholarship to Yale University ,receiving a bachelor's degree in psychology .In 1973.While at Yale university ,he met Lacena ["candy"]Rustin;the couple married in 1975 and had three children.He next attended the university of Michigan ,earning a medical degree in 1977 ,and later Johns Hopkins university .Medical school in Baltimore ,Maryland ,where he completed a residency in neurosurgery .In 1984,after a brief stint as a senior registrar in neurosurgery at the charles Gairdner Hospital ,part of the queen Elizabeth ,medical center i Nedlands ,Western Australia ,Carson became director of pediatric neurosurgery at John Hopkins.He was one of the youngest doctors in the United States to earn such a title.
